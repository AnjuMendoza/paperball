National Paperball Association

Founded in November 2025, Paperball is a fast-paced, strategy-driven sport that blends baseball with Convergint office supplies. What began as a casual game has grown into a full league and a passionate community of players. The NPA celebrates the fun and creativity behind Paperball, offering a fresh take on sports where skill, quick thinking, and a bit of flair make all the difference.

Antonio Mendoza

Antonio Mendoza, often hailed as one of the greatest paperball players in history, has secured an impressive 3 NPA championships, achieving the record for the most in the sport. His remarkable career is characterized by his relentless determination and unrivaled prowess on the clay courts. Mendozas's achievements have solidified his status as an iconic figure in the world of paperball.

Arthur Zanello

Arthur Zanello, an iconic figure in paperball, holds the most recent NPA champtionship title, after defeating Antonio Mendoza in a game 7 series final. Renowned for his elegant playing style and sportsmanship, Zanello's influence extends beyond the field, making him a global ambassador for the sport. His enduring legacy embodies the essence of paperball excellence.
